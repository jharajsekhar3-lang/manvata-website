Est. 1992

Our Story

With over three decades of excellence, Manvata Consultants Pvt. Ltd. began its journey as a consulting firm in 1992 and has since evolved into a dynamic force in film and audio-visual production. From management consultancy to cinematic storytelling, our journey reflects a commitment to growth, creativity, and meaningful impact across industries.

Who We Are

A Legacy of Meaningful Cinema

Manvata Consultants Pvt. Ltd. is a film and audio-visual production company dedicated to creating socially relevant, meaningful, and culturally rooted stories. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Rajesh Gautamkumar Jha, the company has produced acclaimed feature films such as Haati Haati Paa Paa (Bengali), Meghla (Bengali), and The Darling Wife (Hindi), each reflecting a commitment to impactful storytelling and cinematic excellence.

Beyond feature films, Manvata has evolved into a trusted creative partner for leading brands across sectors. The team delivers high-quality visual content, corporate films, ad films, and BTL marketing solutions for clients such as Nuvoco Concreto Cement, JBG Hexa TMT, Shyam SEL TMT, and many more.

From conceptualisation to execution, Manvata is known for producing compelling narratives that elevate brand communication and audience engagement. With a blend of creative vision, technical expertise, and a passion for storytelling, Manvata Consultants continues to push boundaries in film production and brand communication.

Bringing stories, ideas, and identities to life on screen and beyond — that is the Manvata promise, upheld with integrity and artistry since 1992.

The People Behind Manvata

Meet Our Team

Director

Arnab K Middya

Arnab K Middya is a filmmaker by profession who has directed the critically acclaimed feature film Andarkahini, which was screened at 53 National and International film festivals and received 39 awards in various categories from renowned film festivals. His last theatrical released feature film was Sedin Kuyasha Chilo which was successfully screened at theatres for more than 75 days. He is currently working on two feature films Meghla and Haati Haati Paa Paa.

Before his filmmaking career, he was associated with the National Film Development Corporation Limited (NFDC) where he worked on a prestigious campaign for promotion of West Bengal Tourism featuring Shahrukh Khan. He has handled clients like Coal India, Indian Oil, Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Tourism (Govt. of India) and has been associated with marketing and distribution of feature films like Bhaag Milkha Bhaag, LunchBox, Manjhi, Qissa, Tasher Desh and many more.
