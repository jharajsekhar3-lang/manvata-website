Capabilities

What We Do

From the first frame to the final cut — and from concept to campaign — we bring every story to life with purpose and precision.

🎬

Film Production

We craft feature films, short films, and audio-visual content with cinematic excellence and deep cultural roots. Every frame tells a story that matters. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Rajesh Gautamkumar Jha, Manvata has produced acclaimed Bengali and Hindi feature films including Haati Haati Paa Paa, Meghla, and The Darling Wife — each a testament to our commitment to impactful, culturally rooted storytelling. From script development and casting to production and post-production, we handle every aspect of the filmmaking journey with craft and care.

📡

Brand Communication

We deliver high-quality visual content, corporate films, ad films, and BTL marketing solutions for leading brands across sectors. Our clients include Nuvoco Concreto Cement, JBG Hexa TMT, Shyam SEL TMT, and many more. From conceptualisation to execution, we elevate brand communication and drive audience engagement. Our team blends creative storytelling with strategic thinking to produce content that not only looks exceptional but genuinely connects with the target audience and moves them to action. We understand that every brand has a unique story — and we're here to tell it brilliantly.

🤝

Film Sponsorship

We actively seek strategic sponsors and investors for Manvata productions. Partner with us to bring meaningful, culturally rooted stories to audiences across India and beyond. With projects like the upcoming historical epic Raanikaahini — the untold story of India's forgotten freedom fighter spy — and the Bengali drama Meghla currently in development, sponsorship opportunities offer partners significant brand visibility, cultural association, and the rare privilege of being part of a story that will be remembered. We work closely with sponsors to ensure mutually beneficial, creatively integrated partnerships at every level.

🌟

Supporting Productions

We believe in the power of storytelling beyond our own productions. Manvata partners with and sponsors other production houses to help bring their meaningful stories to life on screen. Our support extends from financial investment and creative consultancy to production infrastructure and industry connections. We are committed to nurturing India's independent cinema ecosystem and helping talented filmmakers realise their vision without compromise. If you have a story worth telling and are seeking a creative and financial partner with deep industry expertise, Manvata Consultants is here to help you take it forward.
